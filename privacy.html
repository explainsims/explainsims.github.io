Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 8, 2026  ·  explainsims.github.io

Overview

ExplAIn Sims is a free, open educational site providing physics simulations and teacher tools. This policy explains how information is handled when you use the site, particularly when using features that connect to your Google account.

Short version: We don't collect or store your data. Some tools let you create or copy Google Docs in your own Drive — that content goes directly from your browser to Google. We see nothing, store nothing, and sell nothing.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

ExplAIn Sims has no backend servers, databases, or analytics. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, location)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies (none are set by this site)
  • Any content from files you upload or documents you create

The site is served as static files from GitHub Pages. Nothing you do on this site is logged or recorded by us.

2. Google Sign-In and Google Workspace APIs

Some tools — currently Docs Tab Builder and Chatbot to Docs — use Google Sign-In and Google Workspace APIs (Google Docs and Google Drive) to create or read documents in your Google Drive. This section explains exactly what happens when you use these features.

What permissions are requested:

  • Create and manage Google Docs files — so the tool can create new documents in your Drive.
  • Read-only access to Google Drive — so you can browse and select existing documents to import. We do not modify, delete, or access any files other than those you explicitly select and any new documents the tool creates for you.

What happens with your data:

  • Your OAuth access token is stored in your browser's sessionStorage only — it is never sent to any server other than Google, and is automatically cleared when you close the browser tab.
  • Files you upload (PDFs, Word docs, text files) are read and processed entirely within your browser. Their content is never transmitted to us.
  • Document content is only sent to Google when you explicitly click a button to create or copy a document. It goes directly from your browser to Google's servers.
  • We do not read, store, or retain any of your Google Drive contents, document text, or account information.

What we do not do:

  • We do not store, cache, or log any Google user data.
  • We do not share or transfer Google user data to any third party.
  • We do not use Google user data for advertising or any purpose other than providing the feature you requested.
  • We do not sell Google user data. Ever.

3. Google API Services — Limited Use Disclosure

ExplAIn Sims's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Third-Party Scripts and CDNs

The site loads open-source libraries from third-party CDNs (such as cdnjs.cloudflare.com and googleapis.com) to power features like PDF rendering, graphing, and math typesetting. These CDN providers may log standard HTTP request metadata (IP address, browser type, timestamp) as part of normal server operation. We have no control over and no access to those logs. Please review the privacy policies of Cloudflare and Google for details.

5. Children

This site is designed for educational use by teachers and students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone. Because we collect no data at all, there is no special handling required for younger users.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we add new features that change how data is handled, we will update this policy and revise the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to the project's GitHub repository: github.com/explainsims/explainsims.github.io
